From 2021 to 2024, I am a Junior Fellow at Harvard Society of Fellows; I am mentored by Professor Peter Kronheimer. I received PhD from Stony Brook University in 2021; my thesis advisor is Professor Aleksey Zinger.

My research area is geometry and topology, with is also related to mathematical physics. My previous works are mostly in real and open Gromov-Witten theory, while I also recently got interested in configuration space integrals.
